KongPao-Chicken

Description

Kung pao chicken is Chinese-style stir-fried chicken cubes with dried chili peppers. Itâ€™s a Sichuanese staple that was brought over to America and reinvented for local flavors and tastes. The Sichuanese version is a tad more complex, seasoned with Chinese peppercorns and a large heap of dried chili peppers. The American rendition stays true to most of the original ingredients, but the flavor profile is significantly sweeter and will sometimes include bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Soy sauce (or tamari)
  • Vinegar (rice vinegar or sherry vinegar)
  • Cornstarch
  • Granulated sugar
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Red & green bell peppers
  • Green onions
  • Cashews
  • Dried red chili peppers
  • Fresh garlic & ginger
